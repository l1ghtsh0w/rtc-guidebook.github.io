First Week at RTC

Your first several days at RTC will undoubtedly be among the most difficult. You will be expected to learn quite a lot of material in a short amount of time:

  • basic military bearing:
    • greeting fellow personnel;
    • saluting;
    • wearing your cover;
  • obstacle course;
  • third class swim test.

Your RDCs understand it is a lot of information, and do not expect you to know things right away. After you have attended a couple of classes, however, it will be expected that you know the basics.
