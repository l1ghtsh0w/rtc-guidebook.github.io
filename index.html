About This Book

This book is designed to help you, a new candidate or recruit in the USNSL, survive and make the most of your time here at NSTC. Parts of this book will be OOC while others will be IC.

Using the navigation links to the left, you can choose what you would like to read up on. It is recommended that you start with the Terms and Disclaimer pages, and then move on to the Policies page.
