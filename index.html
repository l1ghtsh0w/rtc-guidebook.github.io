About This Book

This 'book' is designed to help you—a new recruit in the USNSL—survive and make the most of your time here during RTC. Parts of it will be Out-Of-Character while others will be In-Character or Semi-In-Character.

"If it moves, salute it. If not, polish or shine it."
- Dave Gaffer, former USNSL member

Using the navigation links to the left, you can choose what you would like to read up on. However, it is recommended that you start with the Terms and Disclaimer pages, and then move on to the Policies page.

With that being said, welcome to Recruit Training Command with the US Navy in SL. We hope you 'enjoy' your time here.
